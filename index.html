Hi, my name is Lexie Kirsch.

I am a Human Factors Engineer
seeking use-related problems to solve,
usability studies to run, data to analyze,
risks to mitigate, & user experiences to improve.

I graduated from Tufts University in May 2019
with a Masters in Human Factors Engineering and a certificate in Human Factors in Medical Devices and Systems.

At Tufts University, I learned how to design experiments, perform statistical analyses, and visualize information.

Then I worked at Farm Design,
a medical device design consultancy in Hollis, New Hampshire.

At Farm Design, I built upon my academic foundation with direct experience conducting usability assessments and authoring documentation within an ISO 13485-certified quality system. I also co-authored an article featured in AAMI's BI&T that demonstrates how to use the MAUDE and FDA's Recall databases to mitigate risk.

In my spare time, you can find me escaping escape rooms and playing word games :).
